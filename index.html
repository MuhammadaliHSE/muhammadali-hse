About Me

I am a highly dedicated and results-driven Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) Officer with over five years of experience ensuring the safety and well-being of employees across industrial and construction projects in Saudi Arabia, including the Jazlah Water Desalination Plant (JWDC, Jubail) and the King Abdullah Financial District (KAFD, Riyadh).

My commitment to fostering a safe work environment goes beyond compliance – I focus on creating a culture where safety is integrated into every operation. I specialize in hazard identification, risk assessment, safety audits, and employee training. I collaborate with various departments to maintain high safety standards and implement proactive solutions to reduce risks.

With certifications in NEBOSH, ISO 45001, and OSHA, I bring deep knowledge of global HSE practices and a passion for continuous improvement. I aim to make every workplace safer, more efficient, and more environmentally responsible.
